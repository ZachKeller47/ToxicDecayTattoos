ABOUT ME

My name is Elijah Ramos. Born and raised south side Chicago in a beautiful Puerto Rican household. Art has been my passion since I could pickup a pencil.
My speciality is black and grey realism with my own flair along with lettering.
When you book with me, expect quality service in every detail from collaborating your ideas with my expertise to bring your concepts into fruition.
I’ve been professionally tattooing for over 5 years and still have the same drive to perfect the craft as much as when I first started my apprenticeship.
Now let’s create unique pieces and have memorable sessions!
